    Solin, a day trip from Split

    Solin is a town situated northeast of Split on the Adriatic sea and the river Jadro. Being the birthplace of emperor Diocletian, it was important both politically and religiously. It was previously called Salona and it was the capital of the Roman province of Dalmatia. You can imagine its importance for the Roman empire. If you happen to be interested in archaeology or like history, this is a place you must visit.
